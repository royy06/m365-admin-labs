## Microsoft 365 Licensing Comparison

## E1
- Web-based apps only
- Email, Teams, and SharePoint access
- No desktop Office apps

## E3
- Desktop Office apps included
- Advanced compliance and security tools
- Standard enterprise license

## E5
- Advanced security and compliance
- Microsoft Defender features
- Advanced analytics and protection tools

## Summary
E1 = Basic access  
E3 = Standard business use  
E5 = Advanced security and enterprise protection
