# Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) Enforcement

## What is MFA
Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) adds an extra layer of security by requiring users to verify their identity using more than just a password.

## Factors used
- Something you know (password)
- Something you have (phone/app)
- Something you are (biometrics)

## Why MFA is important
- Protects against password theft
- Reduces risk of account compromise
- Required for most enterprise environments

## How it is enforced (Microsoft 365)
- Through Microsoft Entra ID security settings
- Or Conditional Access policies
